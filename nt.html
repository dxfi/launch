Network


Currently Ox only supports four networks, several of them.

• Ethereum

• Binance (BSC)

• Ethereum Testnet (Goerli)

• Binance Testnet (BSC)



Which will be rolled out in stages. In the initial stage we launched the Binance (BSC) and Ethereum (Goerli) testnet networks, this launch is in the testnet stage, or network testing phase.



The next stage includes the launch of the Ethereum mainnet network and the Binance Mainnet (BSC), and this is in the mainnet phase.



And there will be several other mainnet networks available, Arbitrum, OpBNB, Avalanche, Mantle, and Coredao.
