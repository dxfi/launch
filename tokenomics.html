Tokenomics

Tokenomics is the economic mechanism of a cryptocurrency or token that defines the rules for the use and distribution of tokens, and regulates the supply and demand in the market.

Typically used to achieve project goals such as ensuring token price stability, financing project development, incentivizing user participation, and more.
